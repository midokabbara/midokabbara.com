analytics redesign

shopify 2024 senior staff pm
all merchants
#1 pain point

the problem

shopify's analytics was rigid. predefined reports, limited customization. merchants couldn't explore their own questions. this was the #1 analytics pain point.

the old model assumed shopify knew what was important. merchants were stuck in boxes we created. want to see conversion rate by traffic source for the last 90 days? that exact combination didn't exist.

the approach

rather than adding more reports, we went back to first principles: what decisions are merchants trying to make?

the answer: they need to explore their data, not just view reports. this led to three fundamental shifts:

  • customizable dashboards: merchants build their own views
  • exploratory data access: drill into any metric, slice by any dimension
  • shopifyql for power users: query language for advanced analysis

what i built

i owned the redesign from vision to ship:

  • defined product vision: shift from "reports we give you" to "tools to answer your own questions"
  • designed system architecture: customizable dashboards, drag-and-drop metrics, saved configurations
  • integrated shopifyql for power users who want direct data access
  • led cross-functional execution across design, engineering, data platform teams
  • shipped to ALL shopify merchants (not a beta or subset)

the outcome

rolled out to all shopify merchants globally, summer 2024. replaced the entire previous analytics experience. merchants can now answer their own questions without waiting for us to build specific reports.
