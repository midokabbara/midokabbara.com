the pin2 challenge

pwc 2012–2015 kuwait
0→1 created
global flagship csr

the problem

i wanted to create impact outside client work. no csr initiative existed for entrepreneurship education in kuwait.

what i built

created a high school entrepreneurship competition from scratch:

  • designed program, recruited partners, ran operations
  • won sponsorship from leading kuwait businesses and wharton alumni club
  • grew program over three years
  • by year three, kuwait government became partner to take it national

the outcome

pwc adopted the pin2 challenge as their global flagship csr initiative. featured in pwc's 2014 global annual review.
