the problem

amazon wanted to test print-on-demand subscriptions. no existing product, team, or playbook.

what i built

built the entire program from scratch:

  • product definition, engineering, manufacturing partnerships, licensing deals
  • secured licenses with wwe, mtv, pokemon, and music icons
  • led cross-functional team across product, engineering, finance, marketing, and operations

the outcome

multi-million dollar revenue in year one. proved print-on-demand subscription model worked at amazon scale.

also identified that feature velocity was limited by technical architecture, not prioritization. made the case for a new subscription management api. removed the bottleneck to growth. team could ship faster after api was in place.
